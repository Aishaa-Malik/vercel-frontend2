Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: October 10, 2025

Overview

Tori Ate provides a WhatsApp-based appointment scheduling platform designed for doctors, healthcare professionals, turf owners, and other service-based businesses. Our service is delivered as a Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) subscription model with digital access provided immediately upon purchase.

Important Notice: Due to the digital nature of our services and immediate access provisioning, all sales are final. We do not offer refunds for subscription plans once purchased.

No Refund Policy

All subscription plans purchased on Tori Ate are non-refundable. This includes but is not limited to:

  • Monthly subscription plans
  • Quarterly subscription plans
  • Annual subscription plans
  • Any promotional or discounted plans
  • Add-on features or upgrades

Reasons for No Refund Policy

Our no-refund policy exists because:

  • Access to our software platform and features is granted immediately upon payment
  • Digital services cannot be "returned" once accessed
  • We provide detailed plan information, features, and pricing on our website before purchase
  • Free trial or demo sessions are available to evaluate our service before committing to a paid plan

Exceptions

Refunds will only be considered in the following exceptional circumstances:

  • Duplicate payments made in error (verified through transaction records)
  • Technical errors resulting in overcharging
  • Service unavailability for more than 72 consecutive hours due to issues on our end

All refund requests for exceptional circumstances must be submitted within 7 days of the transaction date to support@torriate.com.

Cancellation Policy

You may cancel your subscription at any time through your account dashboard or by contacting our support team at support@torriate.com.

Cancellation Terms

  • Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing cycle
  • You will retain access to all features until the end of your paid period
  • No refunds will be provided for the remaining unused portion of your subscription period
  • No partial refunds for monthly or annual plans cancelled mid-cycle
  • Cancelled subscriptions will not auto-renew for the next billing cycle

Account Deletion

If you wish to delete your account entirely, please contact us at support@torriate.com. Account deletion is permanent and all data will be removed in accordance with our Privacy Policy.

Payment Processing

All payments are securely processed through Razorpay. Payment information is encrypted and we do not store your complete credit card or payment details on our servers.

Payment Issues

If you experience any payment-related issues:

  • First, check your bank account or credit card statement to verify the transaction
  • Contact your credit card company or bank for transaction details
  • If the issue persists, contact us at support@torriate.com with your transaction ID

Subscription Changes & Upgrades

You may upgrade or downgrade your subscription plan at any time:

  • Upgrades: When upgrading to a higher-tier plan, you will be charged a prorated amount for the remainder of your current billing cycle
  • Downgrades: When downgrading to a lower-tier plan, the change will take effect at the end of your current billing cycle. No credits or refunds will be issued for the price difference

Service Interruptions

While we strive to maintain 99.9% uptime, service interruptions may occasionally occur due to:

  • Scheduled maintenance (announced in advance)
  • Emergency maintenance or security updates
  • Third-party service provider issues (WhatsApp, hosting providers, etc.)

Scheduled maintenance does not qualify for refunds. Only extended, unplanned service outages exceeding 72 consecutive hours may be eligible for prorated credits or refunds at our sole discretion.

Chargebacks & Disputes

If you initiate a chargeback or payment dispute with your bank or credit card company without first contacting us:

  • Your account will be immediately suspended
  • Access to all services will be revoked
  • Your account may be permanently terminated
  • We reserve the right to pursue legal action for fraudulent chargebacks

Please contact us at support@torriate.com to resolve any billing disputes before initiating a chargeback.

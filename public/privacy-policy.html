When you subscribe to our service, we collect personal information that you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Name and business name

Information Collected Automatically

When you use our platform, we automatically collect certain information, including:

  • IP address and browser type

As a business owner using Tori Ate, you may upload or input information about your customers, including:

  • Customer names and contact information

You are responsible for obtaining appropriate consent from your customers before uploading their data to our platform.

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our appointment scheduling services
  • To process your subscription payments and manage your account
  • To send appointment reminders and notifications via WhatsApp
  • To provide customer support and respond to your inquiries
  • To send administrative information, updates, and security alerts

  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues or fraudulent activities
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service

Marketing Communications

With your explicit consent, we may send you:

Section 5 - Payment Processing

We use Razorpay as our payment processing partner. All payment transactions are processed securely through Razorpay's PCI-DSS compliant infrastructure.

Important: We do not store your complete credit card or debit card information on our servers. Payment card data is encrypted and securely handled by Razorpay in accordance with Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI-DSS) requirements.

Your purchase transaction data is used only as long as necessary to complete your subscription payment. After processing, your payment card information is not stored by us.

The PCI-DSS standards are managed by the PCI Security Standards Council, a joint effort of major card brands including Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover.

For more information about how Razorpay handles your payment data, please review their privacy policy at https://razorpay.com/privacy/.
